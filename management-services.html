Operations blueprint

Our operating model blends ISO 55000 asset management practices with local content excellence. Engagements are structured into sprints that deliver measurable reliability gains.

  • 01Criticality assessments and risk-ranked maintenance master plans: We conduct comprehensive failure mode and effects analysis (FMEA) to identify critical assets, establishing priority-based maintenance schedules that optimize resource allocation and minimize operational risks.
  • 02Integrated control room buildouts with SCADA, cybersecurity, and outage playbooks: Our control centers feature redundant supervisory control and data acquisition (SCADA) systems, hardened against cyber threats through multi-layer security protocols, complemented by documented emergency response procedures.
  • 03Field execution squads for pipelines, tank farms, terminals, and utility systems: Dedicated teams of OEM-certified technicians execute planned maintenance, emergency repairs, and integrity inspections across diverse infrastructure assets, supported by mobile workshops and real-time communication systems.
  • 04Performance dashboards covering MTBF, availability, and sustainability KPIs: Real-time analytics platforms track mean time between failures (MTBF), overall equipment effectiveness (OEE), energy consumption, emissions, and safety metrics to drive continuous improvement.

Phased Implementation Approach

Phase 1 - Assessment & Planning (Weeks 1-4): Site surveys, asset condition assessments, gap analysis, and development of comprehensive operations & maintenance (O&M) manuals customized to facility-specific requirements.

Phase 2 - Transition & Mobilization (Weeks 5-8): Team deployment, knowledge transfer from incumbent operators, establishment of maintenance routines, spare parts inventory optimization, and vendor relationship activation.

Phase 3 - Stabilization & Optimization (Months 3-6): Implementation of predictive maintenance programs, SCADA integration, performance baseline establishment, and initial process improvements.

Phase 4 - Continuous Improvement (Ongoing): Data-driven optimization, technology upgrades, training programs, and sustainability initiatives to drive long-term value creation.
