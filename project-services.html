EPCI & EPCM

We specialize in the procurement, supply, and installation of essential components for oil and gas projects. Our offerings include valves of sizes up to 12 inches and above, nozzles, flanges, and pipes. Additionally, we provide pipeline systems, process facilities, and equipment to support seamless operations.
Our expertise also extends to project management and construction management. We offer Project Management Consultancy (PMC) services that cover the entire project lifecycle, from design and construction to commissioning and operation. As an Owner’s Engineer, we ensure that projects are executed with strict adherence to safety, scope, cost, schedule, risk management, interface coordination, and contractual compliance.
